🛑 About Pluxee

At Pluxee, we believe every gamer deserves more than just basic gear — they deserve tools that elevate their game. Founded with a passion for gaming and a commitment to accessibility, Pluxee is an online platform delivering premium gaming accessories with fast, free delivery across India. From casual mobile players to dedicated PC warriors, we’re here to make high-quality gaming products affordable and easy to get. We know the frustration of overpriced gear, limited local availability, and delayed delivery — Pluxee is our answer to that. With a growing collection of carefully selected accessories, intuitive shopping, and responsive support, we aim to be your trusted gaming partner, not just a store.
